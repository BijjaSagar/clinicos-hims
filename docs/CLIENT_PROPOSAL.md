## ABDM Compliance

ClinicOS is built to the **Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM)** specification:

- **M1 — ABHA Creation:** Your patients get a unique Health ID via Aadhaar or mobile OTP
- **M2 — Health Information Provider (HIP):** Clinical records shared in FHIR R4 format with patient consent
- **M3 — Health Information User (HIU):** Access records from other providers (hospitals, labs) with patient permission
- **Facility QR Code:** Patients scan a QR at your clinic to link their ABHA to your facility

This means your clinic is future-proof as the government expands digital health mandates.
